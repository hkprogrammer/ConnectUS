Knowing Your Rights:



  1. Red Card rights:
    • Red Cards are cards that identifies you as an immigrant, and which if in a public situation that an officer is trying to approach you, show up your card immediately.

  2. House Search rights:
    • ICE, or the Immigration and Customs Enforcement, or in short the US border of customs and protection. In any case where if they are trying to knock on your door and trying to come in to your house, DO NOT open the door unless if they have a clearance from a judge or department chair. Although, opening your doors does not mean that they have a permission to enter your house.
    • In the same case, where an officer is trying to get into your house, please DO NOT open the door unless if they have a valid warrant or valid search form. you are exercising your constitutional right and will not open the door to them.
